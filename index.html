Welcome to Calculate My Pension


Calculate My Pension is a brand new pension calculator designed to be fast and easy to use. But it is way more than just a simple pension calculator - it is a powerful cashflow modelling tool focused on retirement planning that can help you explore a range of scenarios and understand the possible impact on your retirement plans.

Calculate My Pension also aims to provide all the information you need to know about saving for retirement so you can better understand the results of the calculator. You can find this on the Learn page. There is also a Quiz which aims to identify any gaps in your pensions knowledge that you should fill before using the calculator.

Calculate My Pension is free to use, but also offers premium membership which provides significant additional functionality, including planning your retirement in conjunction with your spouse or partner. Right now, there is a special Early-Bird discount of 50% to our first 100 premium members.

What You'll Learn About Your Pension

Sustainable Retirement Income
Discover how much income you can afford to take in retirement without funds running out, given your current savings and ongoing contributions.
Fund Progression
Observe the future progression of your funds in a chart, growing up to retirement and then declining as you start taking income, targeted to reach zero by the age of your choice.
Retirement Income Breakdown
Analyse the breakdown of your retirement income between different sources in each retirement year, including your state pension, with different sources commencing payment at different ages.
The Effect of Assumptions
Premium members can alter the assumptions for fund growth, inflation and charges. Then see the effect of these changes on fund growth and projected retirement income.
